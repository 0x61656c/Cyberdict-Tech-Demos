Missions are at the core of your Carnegie Cyber Academy Experience.

By playing through training missions, you'll learn the fundamentals of how you can do your part in making the web a safe and inclusive place for everyone. Over the course of your missions, you'll earn badges that prove your experience as a seasoned cyber defender.

There are a wide variety of training missions available in multiple fields. Try to earn as many badges as you can, and before you know it you'll be an experienced cyber defender! You can take the skills you learn from training missions into the real world; they'll help you learn about becoming a good internet citizen, protecting your personal information, how to detect malware and scams, and, most importantly, how to keep yourself and your information safe from attackers. If you're really interested, you can even learn about Cyber careers, and learn about working to protect the internet from cyber criminals.
